About

Welcome to my music page! I’m De Le Greed, a self-taught producer from NY who’s journeyed from a tough childhood to a world of sound. With a guitar gifted by my family, my passion for music ignited. After diving into EDM on various platforms, I discovered something was missing—until Logic arrived and opened up a whole new realm of creativity. Now, I’m crafting tracks that blend my love for EDM and Hip-Hop, drawing inspiration from legends like Muddy Waters to modern artists like Bibio. Explore my music, and let’s connect—this is just the beginning.
